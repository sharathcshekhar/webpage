I have close to 4 years of experience in system software development, which comes from my 3 year stint in the industry before joining the grad school in UB. In addition, I also have successfully completed three summer internships during my grad school.

Amazon.com, Inc.

I worked as a Summer Intern, with the role of software development in Amazon Web Services, Seattle, during the summers of 2013, 2014 and 2015, accumulating close to a year of software development experience. During all three internships, I worked in the Amazon Redshift team, which develops the cloud scale data warehousing solutions. Specifically, I worked in their systems team of database storage layers. I worked on projects which provide LZO compression for the data (summer 2013), database table level backup and restore mechanisms (summer 2014), and backup management tools for smart disk replacement (summer 2015).

NetApp, Inc.

Before starting grad school in UB, I worked as a Software Engineer in NetApp, Bangalore from 2011-2012. I worked in the development team of NetApp V-Series, which are virtualized storage controllers for third party storage arrays.

Cisco Systems, Inc.

After my Bachelor's degree I started my career in software engineering with Cisco Video Tech. Ltd. (formerly NDS Ltd.) and worked there from 2009-2011. Here, I worked on set-top-box middleware and device driver validation tools. I also worked in the NDS Research labs in Paris for three months.
