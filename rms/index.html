Reliable Mobile Systems is a systems lab in University at Buffalo which works on Mobile Systems. It is headed by Dr. Steve Ko, Dr. Karthik Dantu and Dr. Lukaz. It was formed in 2015 and has produced many interesting projects since then. We use Andorid as our platform.
